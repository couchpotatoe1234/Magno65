## Magno65
After weeks of hard work, here it is. The first prototype of the Magno65.
![Screenshot 2026-04-16 at 6.49.17 PM](https://stasis.hackclub-assets.com/images/1776441740199-fgnm2s.png)
It will use N52 Neodymium magnets that are 5mmx2mm in the PCB and on the bottom of the case, allowing it to levitate.
